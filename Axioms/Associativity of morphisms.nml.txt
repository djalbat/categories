Variable C:Category

Variable X:Object
Variable Y:Object
Variable Z:Object
Variable W:Object

Variable f:Morphism
Variable g:Morphism
Variable h:Morphism

Axiom [C, X, Y, Z, W, f, g, h] (CategoryAssociativityOfComposition)
  Suppose 
    f:X ⟶ Y in C
    g:Y ⟶ Z in C
    h:Z ⟶ W in C
  Then
    (f;g);h = f;(g;h) = f;g;h
